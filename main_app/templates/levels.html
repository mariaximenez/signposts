Signpost Levels

Difficulty at the Beginning

The birth of a new venture is an entry into the unknown. New things seem to be rushing upon you. However, chaos is a powerful force if you harness it correctly. Stay calm and be ready to perservere.

Overview

Shallow wells rarely strike water, and shallow minds often come up empty. The ability to keep still and deeply observe strengthens resolve. Discern the difference between what is deep and what is shallow within yourself, and you will be able to distinguish between the two in the outer world as well.

Retreat

All worthy goals meet some resistance. When negative forces predominate, a well-timed retreat can be good for keeping your energy up. Timing is critical. Skillful retreat is a sign of intelligence and strength.

Oppression

As hard as our failures are to accept, they can open our eyes, reawakening a clarity of vision only known by those who have risked and tasted disappointment. The clarity and learning is the silver lining of dark clouds.

Keeping Still

Treat your vitality as if it were a candle in the wind.Protect it as though it were the only light in a dark forest. Avoid external conditions that threaten to snuff out this precious flame.

Fellowship

Bonding only develops if personal interests are subordinate to communal interests such as open communication and empathy. A spirt of cooperation steadies the boat for the good of all. With a unified group solidly behind you, even difficult enterprises can be undertaken without great risk.

Centering in the Truth

Become conscious of the relationship between your heart and the circumstances of your life. Connect with the fundamental wisdom within you, within other people, and within all of nature.

Endurance

True endurance is not based on rigidity, because endurance implies flexibility- not being locked in position. Only by adapting to change can we stay in the race.

Nearing Completion

The situation is incomplete, but the chaos of the past is giving way to order--the goal is in sight. Though the way ahead is unobstructed and the goal is near, a cautious and careful attitude is still important.
